Over 100 bird species spotted on TVS motor factory premises in Hosur

Migratory birds are seen on the TVS motor's factory premises in Hosur in Krishnagiri district. During the recent bird census conducted by the Hosur forest division on behalf of the Tamil Nadu forest department, more than 100 species of birds were identified in 15 waterbodies with the help of over 50 volunteers from the Kenneth Anderson Nature Society (KANS).
